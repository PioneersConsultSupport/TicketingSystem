Microsoft Dynamics 365 finance and operation

Microsoft Dynamics 365 finance and operation, previously know as AX or AXAPTA, is an adaptable business management solution that offers powerful new capabilities to help growing multi-site organizations streamline processes, reduce operational costs, manage compliance and drive informed decision-making.

Microsoft Dynamics 365 is a set of enterprise resource planning (ERP) and customer relationship management (CRM) applications developed by Microsoft. It is a cloud-based platform that can be customized to fit the specific needs of a business. Dynamics 365 includes a wide range of tools for managing financials, supply chain, manufacturing, field service, customer service, and marketing. The platform can be integrated with other Microsoft products like Office 365, Power BI, and Azure to provide a seamless user experience. Dynamics 365 also includes advanced analytics and business intelligence capabilities to help businesses gain insights from their data. Overall, Microsoft Dynamics 365 is a comprehensive solution for businesses looking to streamline operations and improve customer engagement.

In addition to its robust feature set, Microsoft Dynamics 365 offers a number of benefits to users. One major benefit is the ability to access the platform from anywhere with an internet connection. This allows for easy collaboration between team members and the ability to work remotely. Dynamics 365 also provides real-time insights and data visualization tools to help users make better business decisions.

Another benefit of using Dynamics 365 is the ability to scale the solution to meet the needs of the business. As a business grows and evolves, additional modules and functionalities can be added to the platform to support new processes and requirements. Microsoft Dynamics 365 also provides a high level of security and compliance, ensuring that sensitive business and customer data is protected. It is compliant with multiple industry standards such as SOC 1, SOC 2, and HIPAA, and Microsoft regularly updates the platform to meet the latest security standards.

Finally, the platform is supported by a large and active community of developers, consultants, and partners. This means that businesses can easily find support and resources to help them customize and implement Dynamics 365 to meet their specific needs.

Overall, Microsoft Dynamics 365 is a powerful and versatile platform that can help businesses of all sizes streamline operations, improve customer engagement, and drive growth.

WeCare: Health Information System

We Care HIS is a vertical business solution developed on Microsoft Dynamics F&O (Microsoft ERP). We Care HIS is using two different environments in hospitals to realize and simplifies all medical and financial workflow. You can't meet the unique needs of the healthcare industry with generic software that treats all industries equally. At We Care HIS we understand that the healthcare industry. So, We Care HIS is specifically designed for healthcare, which means it offers the broadest, deepest solutions for all organizations that serve the healthcare delivery model.

All data storage and business logic is planned and implemented on Dynamics F&O. Financial working environment is also in Dynamics F&O. For medical working environment We Care HIS has a windows forms application build on .Net Framework, named “We Care HIS Client”.

We Care HIS is not a third-party hospital information management system integrated with AX but is fully developed on AX.

IVF: Health Information System

In-Vitro Fertilization (IVF) is a type of assisted reproductive technology (ART) that can be used to help couples who are struggling to conceive. IVF is a complex process that involves fertilizing an egg outside of the body and then implanting it into the uterus.

An IVF Health Information System (IVF HIS) is a specialized type of HIS that is specifically designed to manage and track the various steps of the IVF process. An IVF HIS can help to streamline the process and ensure that patients receive the best possible care.

Elite Real-Estate: Management System

Elite Real Estate Management System (EREMS) is a specialized software solution for managing and growing a real estate business using Microsoft Dynamics AX. EREMS is a comprehensive and flexible platform that can help real estate professionals streamline their operations and improve their bottom line.

By using Microsoft Dynamics AX as the foundation, EREMS offers a wide range of tools to manage your property portfolio, track and record financials, and provide detailed reporting to aid decision-making. With the ability to integrate with other Microsoft products such as Office 365, Power BI and Azure, EREMS provides a seamless user experience that empowers real estate professionals to make better business decisions.

EREMS also offers features that are specific to the real estate industry, like real estate marketing management, property lease management, and real estate analytics. It also provides advanced analytics and business intelligence capabilities to help businesses gain insights from their data, and also it can track various types of income and expenses like rent, utilities, maintenance, etc. This allows real estate professionals to have a more accurate and detailed picture of their financial performance.
